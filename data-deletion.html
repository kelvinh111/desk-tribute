Data Deletion Instructions

How to Delete Your Data

DESK respects your privacy and provides easy ways to delete your data.

What Data We Collect

DESK is primarily a showcase website and collects minimal data:

  • Basic website usage analytics
  • Information shared through Facebook Login (if used)

Deletion Methods

1. Email Request

Send an email to: [your-email@domain.com]

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Include: Your name and any relevant account information
  • Response time: Within 30 days

2. Facebook Data

For data shared through Facebook:

  • Visit Facebook's Your Facebook Information page
  • Go to "Apps and Websites"
  • Find "DESK" and remove access

3. Browser Data

To clear local browser data:

  • Clear your browser cookies and cache
  • This removes any locally stored website data

What Happens After Deletion

Once we receive your deletion request:

  1. We will confirm receipt within 48 hours
  2. Your data will be deleted within 30 days
  3. You will receive confirmation when deletion is complete
  4. Some data may be retained for legal compliance purposes
